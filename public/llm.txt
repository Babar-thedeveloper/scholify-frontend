# Scholify

> Scholify is Pakistan's #1 free platform for discovering scholarships and internships - national, international, and provincial - with deadline reminders and a free CV builder for students.

Scholify aggregates and hosts scholarship and internship opportunities for Pakistani students. Some listings are managed on-platform (students apply through Scholify and track status); others link out to the provider's official application page ("Direct Apply"). All core features are free, with no paywall.

## Key pages
- [Scholarships](https://scholify.pk/scholarships): searchable directory of national, international, and provincial scholarships. Supports `?search=` queries.
- [Internships](https://scholify.pk/internships): paid, unpaid, remote, and onsite internships for students.
- [AI CV Builder](https://scholify.pk/ai-cv): free CV builder with Europass and Modern templates, exportable to PDF.
- [About](https://scholify.pk/about): mission, story, and team.

## Structured data
- Every page exposes Schema.org JSON-LD: `Organization` and `WebSite` site-wide, `JobPosting` on internship detail pages, and `BreadcrumbList` on listing detail pages.

## Facts
- Audience: students in Pakistan.
- Cost: 100% free.
- Categories: scholarships (national / international / provincial) and internships (paid / unpaid / remote / onsite).
